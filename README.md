# Arpon Heroes Online

Play the Classic Starter Set A as a local pass-and-play or online multiplayer arena game.

## Play

- Pass & Play: share one screen with 2-4 players.
- Private Room: create or join using a six-character code.
- Matchmaking: begins when two players are ready, waits up to two minutes for players three and four, and starts immediately at four.

Arpon Heroes and its artwork are owned by the game's creator.
